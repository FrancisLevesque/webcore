WHO ARE WE?

After a few months out of the spotlight, The District Heat is returning to the stage with their debut album. It only takes a glance to see that their energy and enthusiasm on stage is a reflection of how much they love what they do. With a sound described as everything from the Foo Fighters to The Shins, the band blends their diverse influences into a modern rock feel. Beatroute has described them as "bluesy rock with pop sensibility". This meld of genres has given The District Heat a very unique sound that has qualities everyone can enjoy.
